Welcome

With the expansion of the archives of scientific knowledge, there is a rising importance to understand the science that binds all other forms of sciences together; chemistry. This has led to the development and flourishment of the profession of Chemical Engineering. Chemical engineers are responsible for the availability of modern high-quality materials that are essential for running the world's industrial economy.
