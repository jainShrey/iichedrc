  1. National / International Conferences
    2. IIChE Dehradun Regiona Centre aims to conduct national conferences with a wide range of subjects relevant to latest research and newest technologies in emerging growth areas.
  2. Distinguished Lecture Program
    3. Once in an year, an expert in any field is invited, who elaborates his expertise to the students. The program which is a prestigious one of our regional center is termed as Distinguished Lecture Program. It provides us a stage to interact with the professionals from the industry we belong to and gain something from them.
  3. Industrial Visits
    4. Apart from acquiring theoretical knowledge, IIChE members and student members are keen to conduct industry visits which impart them the practical aspects of chemical engineering. Field trips to ONGC Dehradun , IIP and industrial tours in the country are organized by IIChE .
  4. Awards and prizes
    5. Mainly Eminent Chemical Engineer award and award to Rank holders in the Engineering Colleges.The details will be notified in the site in appropriate time.
